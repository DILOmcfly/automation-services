Frequently Asked Questions

What is n8n and why do you use it?
n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform. Unlike Zapier or Make, it gives you full control over your data, can run on your own servers, and is significantly more cost-effective at scale. It's the professional choice for businesses that care about data privacy.
How fast can you deliver?
Single workflows: 24-48 hours. Multi-workflow systems: 3-5 business days. AI agent setups: 5-10 business days. Timelines depend on complexity and integrations.
Do I need technical knowledge?
No. I handle everything: design, build, testing, deployment, and training. You just tell me what process you want automated.
What happens if something breaks?
Every workflow includes error handling and alerting. If something fails, you're notified immediately and the system recovers automatically. I also offer ongoing support plans.
Can you integrate with my existing hotel software?
Yes. n8n connects to 400+ apps including most PMS systems, booking platforms, CRMs, email providers, and communication tools. If it has an API, we can connect it.
